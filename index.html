ABOUT US

The Billy G Project is a groove-driven pop, rock, and blues band bringing high-energy performances and deep-pocket rhythms to stages across the Low Country. Born out of Charelston, SC area blues jams, BGP has quickly evolved into a strong, four-piece, four-vocalist band featuring strong original music and funky covers. Built on a foundation of tight musicianship, soulful playing, and four-part harmonies, BGP blends classic influences with a modern edge to create a sound that’s both familiar and fresh.

THE BAND

Rich Resnick - Keyboards & Vocals • Billy Gadol - Guitar & Vocals • Rob Tucker - Drums & Vocals • Dave Herr - Bass & Vocals

MUSIC - THE PROJECT

The Billy G Project loves to play! We started by "jamming and hanging" but realized that our adventures needed to become more of a "project". So we started bringing songs to the table from our decades of experience and eclectic tastes to form a gigging band. The result is a fun blend of quality tunes, ranging from sing-alongs to deep listening. We'd love to hang with you as well, whether it's at area clubs, restaurants, parties, or jams! Stay tuned for our show announcements and schedule.
